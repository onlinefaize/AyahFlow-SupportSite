Overview

AyahFlow is a Quran memorization and recitation practice app. This policy explains, in general terms, what information the app may use to operate and improve the service.

Information used by the app

AyahFlow may process learning progress, lesson results, subscription status, device diagnostics, and app usage events that are necessary to operate the app and support users.

If you use recitation or audio-based features, the app may request microphone access so those features can function correctly.

How information is used

Information may be used to deliver app features, maintain progress, troubleshoot bugs, restore purchases, improve stability, and respond to support requests.

Third-party services

AyahFlow may rely on third-party infrastructure and analytics providers to support authentication, storage, purchases, and app reliability. Those providers process data according to their own terms and policies.

Your choices

You can manage app permissions, including microphone access, through iOS Settings. You can also contact support if you have a question about your data or an issue with the app.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, email support@softutopia.com.
