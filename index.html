FAQ

Common questions

Recitation or audio features are not working

Check that microphone permission is enabled for AyahFlow in iOS Settings, then restart the app and try again.

I restored the app or changed devices and my progress looks different

Send us the affected device details and app version so we can review what happened and help you recover the correct state.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the relevant purchase screen in the app and use the restore option. If it does not resolve the issue, email support with your device and iOS version.

I found a translation, content, or display issue

Please email the surah, ayah, screen, and a screenshot if possible. That helps us verify and correct the problem quickly.
